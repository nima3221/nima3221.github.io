Hey! My name is Nikhil Madhu but I go by Nik or Nick.

I am currently an undergraduate at the University of Colorado at Boulder, where I am pursuing a bachelor's degree in Creative Technology and Design in the School of Engineering and Arts.

I am interested in creative storytelling and various creative storytelling mediums. I am interested in 2D and 3D animation, as well as graphic design. I am passionate about filmmaking, both the technical and literary aspects of it. Game design is also something I would like to get into. My dream would be to direct my own film, design my own game, or work in animation.

My hobbies include video games, reading (especially comic books), watching movies, art, playing the piano, and working out.

Thank you for visiting my page and feel free to reach out to me!
