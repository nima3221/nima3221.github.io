How To Make a Protein Shake for Bulkingaccessibility

If you're going to the gym and you're trying to bulk, then you know you need to consume a LOT of calories and protein daily in order to make proper gains. Here is a simple protein shake recipe that's easy to make, easy to consume, and will contribute a significant portion of your daily calorie and protein intake.

Ingredientsgarden_cart

Ingredient Amount
Whey protein powder 2 scoops (roughly 50g of protein)
Oatmeal 1 cup
Milk 1.5 cups
Banana 1 banana
Peanut butter 2 tablespoons

Instructionsegg_alt

1. Put protein powder, oatmeal, and milk into your blender

2. Cut banana into decently small pieces and place it into your blender

3. Blend until the mixture looks liquid and there are no discernable solid chunks

4. Open blender and put peanut butter in, then blend for another few minutes

5. Enjoy!
